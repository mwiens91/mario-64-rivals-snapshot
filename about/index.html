Mario 64 Rivals

Mario 64 Rivals is an open-source web application written by Matt Wiens for competing against your best friends (dawww ) in Mario 64.

How does this work?

Make a record, record it, and submit your time along with a video link. While it's not strictly necessary, it's recommended that you overlay a timer on your record's video.

What are the rules?

There are two classes of records: course records and category records. For course records, collect all six main stars in whatever order you want, and, depending on whether you want to submit a seven star record (instead of a six star record), collect the 100 coin star too. Start the timer when you first enter the course from the course menu and end the timer when you touch the final star. For category records, each has its own rules, which can be found on the respective category's page.

How do I join?

This instance of Mario 64 Rivals is meant to have a closed community, in the sense that all users know each other in person. If you're such a person, contact me and I'll hook you up with an account. If you're not such a person, feel free to host your own instance of Mario 64 Rivals .
